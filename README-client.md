# TechSolutions PK RAG Chatbot

This document is for business or product stakeholders who want to understand what the RAG chatbot does and why it is useful.

## What is this chatbot?

This chatbot is a knowledge-based assistant built on a document about TechSolutions PK. It can answer questions by searching the document and then generating a concise response.

## Why this is useful for clients

- Answers are based on real business information.
- It reduces repeated manual answers for FAQs.
- It can describe services, pricing, process, support, and contact details.
- It stays consistent with the provided document content.
- It avoids inventing answers outside the known information.

## What this chatbot can do

It can answer questions like:

- "What services do you offer?"
- "How much does a chatbot cost?"
- "What is your delivery process?"
- "Do you offer support after delivery?"
- "How can I contact support?"

## How it works (simple terms)

1. A user asks a question.
2. The system looks for the most relevant information inside the saved document.
3. It sends that information to a smart language model.
4. The model writes an answer using only the document content.
5. The answer is shown to the user.

## Why it may say "I don't have that information"

If the question is not answered in the document, the chatbot is programmed to say it does not have the answer and to provide the contact email `hello@techsolutionspk.com`.

This protects the business from giving wrong or invented answers.

## Benefits for the business

- Saves time by automating FAQ responses.
- Keeps information aligned with official business policies.
- Offers 24/7 user access to common company details.
- Improves customer experience with fast, consistent replies.
- Provides a safe fallback when information is not available.

## What is not included

- This is not a general-purpose AI assistant.
- It cannot answer questions outside the stored document.
- It is not a replacement for human sales or support when details are missing.

## Conclusion

This RAG chatbot is a practical tool to deliver reliable information from the TechSolutions PK FAQ document. It is best for sharing service details, pricing, timelines, contact info, and support terms without allowing the model to guess beyond the known content.
